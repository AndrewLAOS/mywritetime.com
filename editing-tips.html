Top Editing Tips for Writing Contests

Enhance clarity, grammar, and storytelling before submitting your work.

Step Away Before Revising

Take a break after writing your draft. Distance gives you a fresh perspective for editing.

Focus on Structure

Check your story arc, paragraphs, and sentence flow. Ensure the narrative makes sense and engages readers.

Proofread for Grammar and Style

Use tools like Grammarly or ProWritingAid, but also read aloud to catch errors automated tools may miss.

Get Feedback

Ask trusted peers, teachers, or writing groups to review your work before submitting.
