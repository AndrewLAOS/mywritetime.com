Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

Thank you for using My Metronome. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information our app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

My Metronome is designed to work without requiring personal information.

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personally identifiable information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, messages, calendars, or other personal data.

Custom Sounds

If you choose to import custom audio files into My Metronome, those files remain stored locally on your device unless you choose to remove them. We do not upload, access, or store your audio files on our servers.

Analytics

At this time, My Metronome does not collect analytics or usage information.

Advertising

My Metronome does not display advertisements and does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties because we do not collect personal information.

Third-Party Services

My Metronome may rely on Apple's iOS operating system and related frameworks necessary for the app to function. Your use of Apple services is governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

My Metronome is suitable for users of all ages. Because we do not knowingly collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Since your data remains on your device, you maintain control over your information. We take reasonable steps to ensure the app follows Apple's security best practices.

Your Rights

Because My Metronome does not collect personal information, there is generally no personal information for us to modify or delete. Any custom sounds or settings stored by the app can be removed by deleting them within the app or uninstalling the application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.
