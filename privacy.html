Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Write Time collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website at https://mywritetime.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Firebase Authentication, we collect your email address and other information provided by the authentication provider.
  • Submissions: If you submit a writing competition, we collect the details of that submission.
  • Analytics: We use services like Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data such as your browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display writing competitions submitted by users
  • Authenticate and manage user access
  • Improve the design, performance, and usability of our site
  • Respond to inquiries or technical issues

3. Use of Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your browser. We use cookies to keep you signed in and to track site usage for analytics purposes. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted third parties such as Firebase and Google Analytics, strictly to operate and analyze the website. These third parties are bound by data protection agreements.

5. Data Security

We take data protection seriously. Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored securely via Firebase services. However, no online system can guarantee 100% security.

6. Children's Privacy

Write Time is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at the address below to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted to this page with a revised effective date.
