5 Tips to Win Your Next Writing Contest

Maximize your chances of success with practical tips for writers of all ages.

1. Carefully Follow All Contest Rules

Every contest has its own rules regarding formatting, word count, eligibility, and submission method. Not following them can disqualify your work immediately.

2. Proofread and Edit Thoroughly

Even the most creative idea can lose impact if the writing is messy. Revise your work multiple times and get feedback from trusted readers.

3. Track Deadlines and Requirements

Keep organized records of all contests you want to enter. Use MyWriteTime Calendar to never miss deadlines.

4. Submit Original Work

Your submission must be your own work. Avoid plagiarism and tailor each entry to the contest’s theme.

5. Study Previous Winners

Analyzing past winning entries helps you understand what judges value, which can inspire your own writing.
