How to Choose the Right Contest for Your Writing

Maximize your success by selecting contests that fit your goals and genre.

Understand Your Genre

Make sure the contest matches your type of writing: fiction, poetry, essay, or scriptwriting.

Evaluate Eligibility

Check age limits, experience level, and residency requirements. Focus on contests you qualify for to save time.

Consider Prizes and Exposure

Some contests offer monetary rewards, while others provide publication opportunities or mentorship. Decide what matters most to you.

Look at Past Winners

Studying previous winners can help you gauge what judges look for.
