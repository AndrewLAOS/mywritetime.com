Why You Should Study Past Contest Winners

Learn from past winners to improve your submissions and gain inspiration.

Understand Judge Preferences

Reading past winners helps you see what judges value, from storytelling style to originality.

Identify Patterns

Notice common themes, genres, or techniques that tend to win. Use these as inspiration, not imitation.

Learn from Success

Analyze why a piece stood out. Was it voice, plot, or emotional impact? Apply lessons to your own writing.

Combine Inspiration with Originality

Take ideas from studying winners and blend them with your unique voice for a compelling submission.
