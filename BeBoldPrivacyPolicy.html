Be Bold Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Be Bold ("we," "our," or "the App") is a confidence-building application designed to help users improve their social confidence through small real-world challenges, reflection activities, and personal growth experiences.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Be Bold collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

Be Bold may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Information you provide when creating an account, such as your name, username, or authentication information.
  • Profile Information: Information used to personalize your experience, such as confidence goals, preferences, selected characters, progress level, XP, completed challenges, and achievements.
  • Journal and Reflection Data: Entries, reflections, feelings, or responses you choose to record inside the app.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with Be Bold, including completed quests, app activity, and progress statistics.

3. Camera Usage

Be Bold uses your device camera to support optional confidence-building features.

Camera access may be used for features such as:

  • Smile or facial expression-based activities.
  • Interactive challenges that use camera input.
  • Features designed to help users practice confidence and self-expression.

Be Bold does not record, store, upload, or share your camera images or video unless explicitly stated and authorized by you.

Camera processing is performed locally on your device whenever possible. You may disable camera access at any time through your device settings.

4. How We Use Information

Information collected by Be Bold may be used to:

  • Provide and improve app features.
  • Personalize challenges and recommendations.
  • Track progress, achievements, and rewards.
  • Maintain app security and reliability.
  • Improve user experience and develop new features.

5. Data Storage

Some information may be stored locally on your device. Certain features may require cloud storage or account services to synchronize your progress across devices.

We take reasonable steps to protect stored information, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Be Bold may use third-party services that help provide app functionality, including authentication, analytics, cloud storage, or infrastructure services.

These services may process limited information according to their own privacy policies.

Examples of third-party services may include:

  • Apple services
  • Cloud database providers
  • Analytics providers

7. Information Sharing

Be Bold does not sell your personal information.

We do not share personal information with third parties except:

  • When necessary to provide app functionality.
  • When required by law.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of users and the service.

8. Children's Privacy

Be Bold may be used by teenagers and young adults. We are committed to protecting the privacy of younger users.

We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal information from children. Parents or guardians who have concerns about a child's information may contact us.

9. Your Choices

You may:

  • Disable camera permissions through your device settings.
  • Request deletion of your account or personal information.
  • Stop using the application at any time.
