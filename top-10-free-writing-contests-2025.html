Top 10 Free Writing Contests of 2025

Boost your writing career by entering these exciting contests with cash prizes — all free to enter!

  1. Craft Short Short Story Contest

    Genre: Flash Fiction (up to 1,000 words)

    Prize: $1,500

    Deadline: August 15, 2025

    Recognized for literary excellence, this contest is free to enter and open to all writers.

  2. Margaret W. Sheldon Fiction Award

    Genre: Fiction

    Prize: $750

    Deadline: August 31, 2025

    Free fiction contest open to emerging writers, judged by established authors.

  3. Aaron Shephard Flash Fiction Prize

    Genre: Flash Fiction (up to 750 words)

    Prize: $750

    Deadline: September 30, 2025

    Annual contest celebrating compelling flash fiction with a generous cash prize.

  4. Academy of American Poets Prizes

    Genre: Poetry

    Prize: Varies by prize; up to $1,000

    Deadline: October 2025 (varies, check website)

    Many free poetry contests for students and emerging poets, administered by universities and the Academy itself.

  5. Nightmare Magazine Fiction Contest

    Genre: Horror, Dark Fantasy, Thriller

    Prize: $500 and publication

    Deadline: October 31, 2025

    Open to writers of dark fiction; contest is free with no entry fee.

  6. National Poetry Competition (UK)

    Genre: Poetry

    Prize: £5,000 (approx. $6,000)

    Deadline: November 15, 2025

    One of the most prestigious poetry contests worldwide; no entry fee for poets aged under 18.

  7. Scholastic Art & Writing Awards

    Genre: Multiple (Fiction, Poetry, Nonfiction, Scriptwriting, etc.)

    Prize: Scholarships and national recognition

    Deadline: December 6, 2025

    Open to students in grades 7–12; free to enter via school or local affiliates.

  8. StorySouth Million Writers Award

    Genre: Short Story

    Prize: $1,000

    Deadline: December 15, 2025

    Celebrates the best short stories published online each year; free to enter through nominated stories.

  9. Stoker Awards (Horror Writers Association)

    Genre: Horror

    Prize: Recognition and industry prestige

    Deadline: December 31, 2025 (entry via published works, free for members)

    Prestigious awards for horror writers; check eligibility and entry details.

  10. Writer’s Digest Annual Writing Competition

    Genre: Multiple categories including poetry, short story, nonfiction

    Prize: Up to $5,000

    Deadline: September 30, 2025

    Highly respected contest; entry fees usually apply but sometimes free promotional periods — check site for updates.
