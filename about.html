About MyWriteTime

Connecting writers to opportunities and resources worldwide.

Founder’s Note

Hi! I’m Andrew, a 14-year-old writer and poet, and I created MyWriteTime to help fellow writers find contests and grow their craft. Our mission is to make writing opportunities accessible to everyone, no matter your experience level.

Our Mission

At MyWriteTime, we believe every writer deserves access to opportunities that can help them grow. We curate a wide range of writing competitions, contests, and resources so writers of all ages and skill levels can discover the right platform to showcase their voice.

Why We Exist

Many talented writers struggle to find the right contests or feel overwhelmed by scattered opportunities online. Our platform brings everything into one place—organized, searchable, and tailored to support your growth.

Who We Serve

We serve aspiring authors, poets, essayists, screenwriters, and storytellers around the world. Whether you’re a student entering your first contest or a professional looking for prestigious competitions, MyWriteTime is here for you.
